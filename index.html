About Pachrang

Pachrang is a vibrant celebration of authentic Indian street food, bringing the colorful flavors of India's bustling streets to your doorstep. Our name "Pachrang" means "five colors" in Hindi, representing the diverse and colorful palette of Indian cuisine.

Every dish at Pachrang is crafted with traditional recipes and fresh ingredients, ensuring an authentic taste experience with every bite. From tangy golgappas to savory bhel puri, we bring you the true essence of Indian street food.
